Why I like the movie

This movie makes me think of the times when I played football and how I enjoyed spending time playig the game with my teammates and friends. I enjoy the story and really like the lessons that can be learned from how people may have lived in that time.

Favorite Actor

My favorite actor in this movie an many others is Denzel Washington. Denzel has a great talent to become his role and bring the audience along for the ride.
